# GitDigital OpenCollective Roadmap

**Last Updated: March 15, 2025**  
**Next Review: June 2025**

## Vision

To build the most transparent, community-governed funding ecosystem for open source cryptography, enabling sustainable development of the Aurora framework and beyond.

## Strategic Goals 2025-2026

1. **Scale funding** – Grow annual budget to $1M by end of 2025
2. **Expand grants** – Fund 50+ projects across all tracks
3. **Strengthen governance** – Implement liquid democracy features
4. **Improve transparency** – Real-time budget dashboards
5. **Build community** – 1,000+ active contributors
