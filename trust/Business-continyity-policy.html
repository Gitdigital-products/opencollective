📋 Business Continuity Policy

Version 1.0 · Last updated: January 2026
GitDigital Products

1. Purpose

This policy establishes the framework for ensuring critical business functions can continue during and after a disruption. It applies to all systems and personnel supporting the zkSolana KYC Compliance SDK.

2. Scope

The policy covers:

  • Core infrastructure (RPC nodes, indexers, build pipelines).
  • Development and release processes.
  • Communication channels with users and stakeholders.

3. Recovery Objectives

  • Recovery Time Objective (RTO): 4 hours for critical services.
  • Recovery Point Objective (RPO): 1 hour of data loss maximum.

4. Incident Response Integration

Business continuity activation is triggered by the Incident Response team when an incident is classified as Level 2 or higher. See the Incident Response Policy for details.

5. Plan Maintenance

This policy is reviewed quarterly and tested through simulated exercises twice per year.
