# GitDigital OpenCollective Governance Model

**Version 1.0 | Last Updated: March 2025**

## Overview

GitDigital Products operates under a **community-first governance model** designed to ensure transparency, accountability, and sustainable growth. This document outlines the decision-making structure, roles, responsibilities, and processes for managing the OpenCollective funds.

## Core Principles

1. **Radical Transparency** – All financial decisions, transactions, and discussions are public
2. **Community Ownership** – The community guides project direction
3. **Meritocracy** – Influence earned through contributions
4. **Sustainability** – Long-term financial health prioritized
5. **Inclusivity** – Diverse perspectives welcomed and respected
