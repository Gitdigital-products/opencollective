### Approval Matrix

| Role | Micro | Small | Medium | Large | Major |
|------|-------|-------|--------|-------|-------|
| Finance Committee Member | - | Approve | - | - | - |
| Finance Committee (group) | - | - | Approve | - | - |
| Core Team | - | - | - | Approve | - |
| Core Team + Community | - | - | - | - | Approve |

## Timing

- **Submission to first review**: Within 3 business days
- **Review to decision**: Within 5 business days
- **Approval to payment**: Within 5 business days
- **Total typical time**: 5-10 business days

## Disputes and Appeals

If an expense is rejected:

1. **Clarification** – Reviewer explains reason
2. **Resubmission** – Submitter can address issues
3. **Appeal** – If still disputed, appeal to Core Team
4. **Final decision** – Core Team decision is binding

## Recurring Expenses

For regular expenses (monthly stipends, subscriptions):

1. Initial approval as standard expense
2. Set up as recurring in Open Collective
3. Reviewed quarterly for continued need
4. Can be cancelled with 30 days notice

## Tax Considerations

- **US contributors**: May receive 1099 if >$600/year
- **International contributors**: Responsible for own taxes
- **GitDigital does not**: Provide tax advice, withhold taxes

## Fraud and Abuse

Submitting fraudulent expenses will result in:
- Immediate rejection
- Permanent ban from future expenses
- Public disclosure (if confirmed)
- Potential legal action

## Transparency

All approved expenses are:
- Listed on Open Collective
- Aggregated in monthly reports
- Audited annually
- Available for public review
