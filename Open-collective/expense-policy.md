# GitDigital Expense Policy

**Version 2.1 | Last Updated: March 2025**

## Overview

This document outlines the policy for submitting and approving expenses from GitDigital's Open Collective funds. Our goal is to fairly compensate contributors while maintaining transparency and fiscal responsibility.

## Guiding Principles

1. **Fair compensation** – Contributors should be fairly paid for their work
2. **Transparency** – All expenses are publicly visible
3. **Accountability** – Expenses must be legitimate and reasonable
4. **Sustainability** – We spend responsibly to ensure long-term viability
5. **Inclusivity** – Policy applies equally to all contributors

## Who Can Submit Expenses?

- Core Maintainers
- Grant recipients (for approved grants)
- Contributors with approved bounties
- Vendors providing services to GitDigital
- Community members with pre-approved expenses

## Types of Expenses

### Eligible Expenses

| Category | Examples | Documentation Required |
|----------|----------|------------------------|
| **Development work** | Coding, review, testing | GitHub PR/issue links |
| **Documentation** | Writing, editing, translating | Links to published docs |
| **Design** | Graphics, UI/UX, branding | Design files or links |
| **Community** | Event organization, moderation | Event details, screenshots |
| **Travel** | Conference attendance (pre-approved) | Receipts, itinerary |
| **Infrastructure** | Cloud services, domains | Invoices |
| **Software** | Tools, licenses | Receipts |
| **Education** | Courses, materials | Completion certificates |
| **Bounties** | Completed bounty tasks | Bounty issue link |

### Partially Eligible Expenses

| Category | Policy | Approval Required |
|----------|--------|-------------------|
| **Hardware** | 50% of cost, max $1,000 | Core Team |
| **Co-working space** | Up to $200/month | Finance Committee |
| **Internet/phone** | Up to $50/month | Finance Committee |

### Ineligible Expenses

- Personal expenses not related to GitDigital
- Alcohol (except at community events with approval)
- Luxury items
- Gifts (except contributor swag)
- Political contributions
- Donations to other organizations

## Expense Limits

| Tier | Amount | Approval Required | Documentation |
|------|--------|-------------------|---------------|
| Micro | $0 - $100 | Automatic (if compliant) | Receipt or link |
| Small | $101 - $500 | Finance Committee member | Receipt required |
| Medium | $501 - $2,000 | Finance Committee | Receipt + justification |
| Large | $2,001 - $5,000 | Core Team | Receipt + detailed justification |
| Major | $5,001+ | Core Team + public comment | Full proposal |

## Submission Process

### Step 1: Prepare Documentation

Gather required documentation:
- **For work**: Links to PRs, issues, or deliverables
- **For purchases**: Receipts or invoices
- **For travel**: Boarding passes, hotel bills
- **For services**: Contracts or agreements

### Step 2: Submit via Open Collective

1. Go to [opencollective.com/gitdigital](https://opencollective.com/gitdigital)
2. Click "Submit Expense"
3. Fill in:
   - Description (clear and specific)
   - Amount (in USD)
   - Category
   - Payout method (PayPal, bank, crypto)
4. Attach documentation
5. Submit

### Step 3: Review

1. **Automated check** – Is it complete? Is documentation attached?
2. **Compliance check** – Does it meet policy?
3. **Approval workflow** – Routes based on amount/category
4. **Questions** – Reviewer may request clarification

### Step 4: Payment

Once approved:
- Processed within 5 business days
- Paid via selected method
- Notification sent upon completion

*Rates are guidelines; specific projects may have negotiated rates.*

### Fixed Bounties

Bounties are set per issue and posted with:
- Description of task
- Acceptance criteria
- Bounty amount
- Deadline (if any)

### Travel Reimbursement

| Item | Policy | Limit |
|------|--------|-------|
| Flights | Economy class | Actual cost |
| Hotels | Standard accommodation | $200/night |
| Meals | Per diem | $50/day |
| Local transport | Public transit/Uber | Actual cost |
| Conference tickets | Registration fee | Actual cost |

*All travel must be pre-approved.*
